Tip about Arequipa
by Arsinoe Sig
Visiting Arequipa, you can take in the spectacular neo-Renaissance Cathedral, the impressive Santa Catalina Convent, colonial mansions and museums, all of which are reason enough to visit Arequipa. On the outskirts of the city one can find many other attractions. But, also you can experiment their gastronomy. The better moment and a particularly special moment to visit Arequipa is the Founding Day of the city, (ask for their sweeties) a major festival which takes place annually in mid-August and includes colourful processions, Peruvian music and dancing, beauty pageants, and even sporting events.

Tip about Manaus
by Marion Bon
I went to Manaus more than once and it is one of my favorite Brazilian cities,because it is in the heart of the Amazon forest and it still is exotic ,full of native Indians and the giant river,whose other margin one cannot even see..the Amazon. Also extremely interesting are the "navio gaiolas"..literally translated" bird cage ships " that do ALL the transport of people and goods,since there are practically no roads.. It is worth spending a week end in a jungle lodge,where the real owners are the jungle animals and you are the guest..!Ariau Tower has all the rooms in the trees and you can watch all the animals outside..from monkeys to iguanas,to macaws ,to spiders etc.
